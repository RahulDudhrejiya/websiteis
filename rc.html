Refund & Cancellation Policy

Refund Policy

Thank you for shopping at Hotel Booking Website. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, please ensure that:

  • The item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it.
  • The item must be in the original packaging.
  • The item needs to have the receipt or proof of purchase.

Refund Process

Once we receive your item, we will inspect it and notify you that we have received your returned item. We will immediately notify you on the status of your refund after inspecting the item.

If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your credit card (or original method of payment). You will receive the credit within a certain amount of days, depending on your card issuer's policies.
